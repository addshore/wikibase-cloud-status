Wikibase cloud status

Showing you the last 7 days of data collected at regular intervals for the wikibase.cloud platform from a series of checks

You may also be interested in https://wmde.github.io/wikibase-cloud-status/ for the official platform status.
Code and data by addshore

General

  • Servies are checked regularly, mostly every 1 min, elastic every 5 mins, for a general 200 status, and response time.
  • elastic and queryservice are also checked for valid results.
  • Green indicates OK, red is bad!

Wikibase cloud status

Showing you the last 7 days of data collected every 1 minuite for the wikibase.cloud platform from cloudflare workers

You may also be interested in https://wmde.github.io/wikibase-cloud-status/ for the official platform status.

Checks include:

  • Response time and 200 status of all services
  • Successful query of the sparql endpoint
  • Successful search for an item
  • Maxlag < 10
